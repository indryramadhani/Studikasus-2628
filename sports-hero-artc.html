Indonesia Wins Overall Champion Title at 2025 Southeast Asia Modern Pentathlon Championship

Buletin News | May 15, 2025

BULETIN NEWS ‚Äî Indonesia is the overall champion of the 2025 Modern Pentathlon Southeast Asian (MPSEA) Championships at the Air Force Academy Complex, Yogyakarta after collecting 17 medals.

An extraordinary and very proud achievement, said the General Chairperson of the Indonesian Modern Pentathlon Central Board (PP MPI) Purwoko Aji Prabowo in a written statement in Jakarta, Thursday.

In the final day of matches on Wednesday (15/4), Indonesia added six gold medals in the triathlon event, which competed in six categories, namely U-17 men and women, U-19 men, junior women, senior men and mixed relay.

In total, the Red and White squad collected 17 gold medals, 6 silver and 2 bronze, from the championship series which took place since May 10 with participants from a number of Southeast Asian countries.

Second place was taken by the Philippines (1 gold, 6 silver and 2 bronze), Vietnam (1 gold, 5 silver and 2 bronze), Malaysia (2 silver, 5 bronze), Singapore (1 silver and 3 bronze), Thailand (6 bronze), and Timor Leste (0).

Purwoko thanked the Indonesian athletes and team officials who had fought hard to raise the Red and White flag on the international stage and make Indonesia's name famous.
