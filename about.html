Who We Are?

Buletin News — is an independent news portal committed to delivering fast, factual, and trustworthy information. Since our founding in 2020, we have aimed to become a leading source of news for the Indonesian public, covering local, national, and international events.We believe that quality journalism is the foundation of democracy. That’s why we continue to grow by offering balanced, accurate reporting while upholding journalistic ethics.

Our Team

Currently, Buletin News powered by a passionate team of 30 individuals working together across various roles—journalists, editors, photographers, developers, and designers. The team is led by Indry Ramadhani Islamiyah, who serves as the editor-in-chief and founder of the platform. Indry strives to deliver accurate and meaningful content to readers every day. I’m always open to collaboration, feedback, and new ideas. Don’t hesitate to reach out if you want to connect!
