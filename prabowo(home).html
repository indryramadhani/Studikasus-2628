When Prabowo Affirms He Is Not Jokowi's Puppet

Buletin News | May 6, 2025

BULETIN NEWS ‚Äî President of Indonesia, Prabowo Subianto, has firmly denied allegations suggesting he is a "puppet president" controlled by his predecessor, Joko Widodo. This assertion comes amid circulating rumors of a "dual leadership" within the current administration.

During a plenary cabinet meeting at the Presidential Office in Jakarta on Monday, May 5, 2025, President Prabowo addressed these speculations directly. They say I'm a puppet president, that I'm controlled by Mr. Jokowi, as if he calls me every night. I must state that this is not true, Prabowo declared.

He acknowledged that he often consults with Jokowi, seeking advice and insights from his ten years of experience leading the nation. However, Prabowo emphasized that these consultations do not equate to control or manipulation.

The rumors of dual leadership have been fueled by the continued influence of Jokowi in political affairs and his close relationship with President Prabowo. Nonetheless, Prabowo's recent statements aim to clarify his autonomous role and decision-making authority as the nation's leader.

This development underscores the importance of clear communication within the government to dispel misinformation and affirm the integrity of Indonesia's leadership structure.
