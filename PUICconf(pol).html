19th PUIC Conference Held in Jakarta

Buletin News | May 15, 2025

BULETIN NEWS ‚Äî From May 12 to 15, 2025, Indonesia hosted the 19th Session of the Parliamentary Union of the OIC Member States (PUIC) at the Parliament Complex in Senayan, Jakarta. The conference, themed "Good Governance and Strong Institutions as Pillars of Resilience," marked the 25th anniversary of the PUIC since its establishment in 1999. It brought together over 450 delegates from 37 OIC member countries and observer nations.

President Prabowo Subianto officially opened the conference, emphasizing the importance of unity among Islamic nations and the role of strong institutions in achieving resilience amid global challenges. He stated, "Today, I am speaking with representatives of one-fourth of the world's population. You are all representatives of all Muslims from Muslim countries in the world."

House Speaker Puan Maharani highlighted Indonesia's commitment to supporting Palestinian independence and advocated for increased women's participation in public decision-making processes. She held bilateral meetings with parliamentary leaders from Algeria, Bahrain, Oman, and the Czech Republic to discuss these issues

The conference concluded with the adoption of the Jakarta Declaration, encompassing 17 statements reflecting the collective resolutions of the member states
