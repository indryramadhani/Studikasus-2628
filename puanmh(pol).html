Puan Maharani Meets Czech Parliament Leaders

Buletin News | May 13, 2025

BULETIN NEWS ‚Äî The Speaker of the House of Representatives, Puan Maharani, held a bilateral meeting with a number of state parliamentary leaders who were present on the second day of the Parliamentary Union of the OIC Member States (PUIC) or conference of the Parliamentary Union of countries that are members of the Organization of Islamic Cooperation (OKI) at the DPR building.

In a bilateral meeting with the leaders of the Algerian, Bahrain, and Oman state parliaments who are members of the OIC, Puan emphasized support for Palestine and Pakistan-India peace. The meeting was held in the Delegation Room of the DPR, Senayan, Jakarta, Tuesday, May 13.

Apart from the three countries, Puan also met the leaders of the Czech parliament. Meanwhile, the Czech Republic is not a PUIC member country but is present as an observer country at the 19th PUIC event in Indonesia.

We just held a bilateral meeting with the leaders of Algerian parliament, Bahrain, Oman as well as the Czechs. And the Czechs came as observers, said Puan.

At the bilateral meetings, Puan was accompanied by the Deputy Chairperson of the DPR's Inter-Parliamentary Cooperation Agency (BKSAP), Irine Yusiana Roba Putri, and Member of the DPR BKSAP Gilang Dhielafararez, and Mufti Anam. Bilateral meetings with the 4 leaders of the friendly countries were held separately.
